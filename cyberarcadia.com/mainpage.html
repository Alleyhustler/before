3/4/2025: I changed the background again, LOL. All of the GIMP concrete texture tutorials I could find were 12+ years old and didn't apply to the current version, so I just sort of had to figure it out. Basically: new image, filter > render > noise > plasma, mess around with the turbulence slider until it has enough turbulence, colors > desaturate > desaturate, new layer, fill the new layer with dark gray and set it on top, play around with different layer modes until you get an effect you like, CTRL+M to merge, filter > map > tile seamless, Filter > Edge Detect > Image Gradient, done.
